Shipping Policy
Processing and Delivery:
At AR Fragrances, we understand the excitement of receiving your favorite scents promptly. Our dedicated
team strives to process and dispatch orders swiftly, ensuring you experience the joy of your chosen fragrances
as soon as possible.
Processing Time:
Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after confirmation of payment.
Delivery Time:
Our standard delivery time is estimated between 3-5 working days. We aim to get your
cherished fragrances to your doorstep promptly, allowing you to indulge in the luxury of our scents without
unnecessary delays.
Delays and Special Circumstances:
While we make every effort to adhere to our standard delivery timelines, unforeseen circumstances such as
road blockages or public holidays may impact delivery schedules. In such cases, we appreciate your
understanding as it may take a bit longer to receive your order.
Shipping Partners:
AR Fragrances partners with reliable and reputable courier services to ensure the safe and timely delivery of
your packages. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number, allowing you to monitor the
journey of your fragrances until they arrive at your doorstep.
International Shipping:
Currently, AR Fragrances operates within Pakistan. For international orders, please contact our customer
service for personalized assistance regarding shipping options, costs, and delivery times.
Order Tracking:
Track the journey of your fragrances in real-time! Once your order is dispatched, we will provide you with a
tracking number via email. You can use this number to monitor the status of your delivery.
AR Fragrances appreciates your trust in our brand, and we are committed to providing you with a seamless
and delightful fragrance shopping experience. Thank you for choosing AR Fragrances!
