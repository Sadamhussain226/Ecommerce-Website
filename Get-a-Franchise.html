Own A Franchise
Note: You must have 1.5-2 Million PKR for this project
Franchise Fee for All over Pakistan will be divided in 3 Tiers
1: Tier 1 franchise ( Karachi, Lahore, ISB, RWP) will given in 10 lacs
2: Tier 2 franchise ( GUJ, MUL, FSD, PWR, HYD ETC) will be given in 7.5 lacs
3: Tier 3 franchise ( QUETTA, TAXILA, AND FARFETCH) will be given 5 lacs
License will be granted for 5 year and can be renewed with same or different terms agreed mutually
Franchise Agreement points by company!
- Company will provide stock cash to cash without credit at 35% discount on Retail Price.
- Company will get 4% royalty on gross sales monthly.
- Contract will be auto renewed yearly.
- Franchise owners must have 1-2 year agreement with shop/kisok owner/landlord.
- License fee is non-refundable. - No other product else then A. Fragrances should be sale on shop.
- Shop renovation expenses and stock display along with inventory will be managed by the Franchise owner.
- Camera access must be given to company
- POS software will be provided by company
- More terms can be discussed at the time of signing the agreement!
What company will be providing?
Shop design along with Staff training and inventory management and shop will be considered as company
shop, any help in Ads and sales, stock fulfillment and complete shop management training, POS software will
be provided by company
Why are we charging 4% Royalty?
Royalty is important for company to run operation and provide after sales services to franchise and we will
using that amount in advertisement of shop on monthly basis
What will be Gross sales?
Gross sales will depend on location and footfall of the area where the shop will be located, average Gross
sale will be around 1 Million to 2 million.
What will be the net profit to the franchise owner?
It will be around 10-15% of gross sales after all expenses.
How much area is needed for a shop to Build?
Shop must have area of 80+ SQ FT
